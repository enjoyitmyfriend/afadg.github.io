About the Qin Dynasty

The Qin Dynasty was the first centralized feudal dynasty in Chinese history, ruling from 221 to 207 BCE.

Founded by Qin Shi Huang (Ying Zheng), it unified China after the Warring States period, standardized writing, currency, weights and measures, and built the Great Wall and Terracotta Army.

Though short-lived (only 15 years), it laid the foundation for China's imperial system for over 2000 years.
