History of NAZI in WWII

Nazi Germany, formally known as the Third Reich, played a major role during World War II. Under the leadership of Adolf Hitler, Nazi Germany pursued policies of extreme nationalism, racism, and anti-Semitism.

Nazi Germany carried out large-scale military expansion and launched a series of aggressive wars, bringing great disasters to the world. Its genocide policy, especially the Holocaust against the Jews, is one of the darkest chapters in human history.
