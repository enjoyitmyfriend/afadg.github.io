Hogwarts is divided into: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw and Slytherin, symbolizing courage, loyalty, wisdom and cunning respectively. Each college was founded by a different founder and has unique values and identity. Despite the rivalry between colleges, students often show solidarity in the face of threats, reflecting the spirit of their respective colleges.
