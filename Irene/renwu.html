Personage

Name：Harry Potter

Graduate Institutions：Hogwarts Gryffindor

Main achievement: Killing the most evil Dark wizard of all time

Harry Potter, an ordinary and mysterious teenager, is the son of James and Lily Potter. He is famous for his defeat of Lord Voldemort, earning him the name "The Who Man". Harry has black hair and green eyes, a brave and kind personality, and is fiercely loyal to his friends. He studies magic at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and faces many challenges with the help of his friends. Harry is not only intelligent, but also dares to sacrifice himself, and eventually becomes a hero in the wizarding world.

 Name：Hermione Granger

Graduate Institutions：Hogwarts Gryffindor

Hermione Granger has a brilliant mind, is calm when things go wrong, and likes to read. Above all, she has a kind and helpful heart. Help Harry Potter and RON Weasley unlock the secrets of Nicolas Flamel and help Harry Potter defeat Lord Voldemort. She is a Muggle-born witch who becomes close friends with Harry and RON and has many adventures together at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Known for her academic excellence and strict adherence to the rules, Hermione's knowledge and skills play a key role in the fight against the forces of darkness.

Name：Ron Weasley

Graduate Institutions：Hogwarts Gryffindor

RON Weasley is known for his loyalty, humor, and sometimes awkward personality traits. He was one of Harry's first friends at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, and the two almost hit it off. RON comes from a pure-blood magical family but is relatively poor, which makes him bring a unique perspective to his friendship with Harry and Hermione. He supports Harry not only with magic, but also with emotion in life. Though RON sometimes feels eclipsed by Harry's light, his courage, loyalty, and sacrifice are integral throughout the story.

Name：Draco Malfoy

Wand: Hawthorn unicorn hair, 10-inch elder wand

Graduate Institutions：Hogwarts Slytherin

Identity; Slytherin Quidditch Seeker, prefect, member of the Investigative Team, Death Eater

Draco Malfoy was born into a pure-blood wizarding family and was proud of his aristocratic background and wealth. Draco's behavior at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is often arrogant and biased, and his adherence to the concept of "pure blood" brings him into conflict with the main character's party. Although he primarily plays the role of the villain in the story, as the series progresses, the reader also sees his inner contradictions and fears, as well as his struggle against the oppression of the dark forces. Draco's character deeply explores the relationship between choice and origin, and how individuals can find their way through difficult situations.

Name：Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore

Graduate Institutions：Hogwarts Gryffindor

Major accolade: The greatest headmaster Hogwarts has ever had

Weapon: Elder Wand (also known as the Elder Wand, one of the Deathly Hallows)

Albus Dumbledore is one of the most influential and intelligent characters in the Harry Potter series. As headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizarding, he is portrayed as a charismatic, visionary, and moral leader. Dumbledore is not only Harry's key guide and supporter in the fight against Voldemort, but also the moral compass of the entire wizarding community. His background and past are filled with tragedy, including loss of loved ones and past missteps, which shaped his character and later decisions. Although Dumbledore dies in the story, his wisdom and influence continue throughout the series as a central force driving the plot.

Name：Minerva McGonagall

Graduate Institutions：Hogwarts Gryffindor

Minerva McGonagall is an important figure at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, serving as Vice-principal and head of Gryffindor House. She has a reputation for being strict and fair, demanding but caring of her students. She excelled at transfiguration and was a powerful and respected witch.

Professor McGonagall is known for her strong will and strong sense of justice. She treats her students fairly and selflessly, and has no tolerance for violations of school rules. However, she also often shows a warm and humorous side at key moments, winning the love of her students. Her office is a typical chaotic area that reflects her busy and full life.

Name：Severus Snape

Main identities: Member of the Order of the Phoenix, former Death Eater, double agent, Professor, Headmaster of Hogwarts, former Master of Slytherin House

Graduate Institutions：Hogwarts Slytherin

Who: Headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizarding after Dumbledore's death, founder of the Order of the Phoenix, Slytherin Quidditch Seeker, prefect, member of the Investigative Squad, and Death Eater

Severus Snape was a talented wizard who served as Professor of Potions and head of Slytherin House. Snape's personality is cold and sharp, and he has an obvious bias against Harry Potter, but there are deep reasons behind it. His heart is full of contradictions and struggles, which are gradually revealed later in the series. Snape's loyalty and sacrifice adds a profound layer to the whole story, making him one of the most memorable characters.

characteristics of personality：Kind, with a strong sense of justice
