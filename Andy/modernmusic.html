Strumify is a conceptual platform designed for music learners, particularly focusing on instruments like guitar, mandolin, and ukulele. It aims to provide interactive lessons, AI-driven personalized learning, and a supportive community for users. Strumify combines innovative teaching techniques with digital tools to make learning accessible and enjoyable for musicians at all skill levels. The project envisions a space where users can improve their skills, share progress, and connect with other enthusiasts.
